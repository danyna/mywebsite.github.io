Rise

Rise was imagined into existance by Percy Katt and Maggie Pie in 1987, they wanted to find a way to keep london healthy in a way that was enjoyable.

We are a company that is commited to bringing parkour and movement skills to london, and making it availabe to everyone. Through movement we wish to introduce a new exciting way to keep fit, we teach practical movement that can be easily translated into everyday life.
